PARQUET MAINTENANCE OIL

Regular oil maintenance helps protect your wooden floor and extend its lifespan. This service is ideal for previously oiled floors.

We apply a professional maintenance oil that nourishes the wood, restores its natural tone, and strengthens the surface.

Service Includes:

  • Cleaning and preparation of the surface
  • Application of professional-grade maintenance oil
  • Buffing and absorption time
  • Safe to walk on after a few hours

Recommended every 12-24 months depending on usage and wear.
